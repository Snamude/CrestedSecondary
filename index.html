About Us

Welcome to Crested Secondary School Kampala

Since our founding in 1995, our mission has been constant: to build a rigorous scholastic environment. We provide our learners with daily, immersive growth opportunities—whether serving in local communities, leading student committees, engaging in choir performances, or competing on regional playing fields.

We operate a modern, inclusive learning framework that is fully compliant with the national Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) curriculum standards, combining traditional academic rigor with vital computer literacy skills.

School Discipline

Code of Conduct

At Crested Secondary School, we strongly believe in moulding self-reliant, morally upright, and law-abiding future leaders. Learn more about our interactive code of conduct.

All students must strictly report to class by 7:30 AM every morning. Attendance of early lessons, prep classes, and weekly school assemblies is compulsory. Latecomers will face disciplinary sessions.

English remains the strictly enforced language of instruction and general communication on campus. Peer interaction must be respectful, peaceful, and support the mixed school co-existence principles.

Personal smartphones, tablet devices, and mobile phones are strictly prohibited within the boarding houses and classrooms. Computer literacy training is strictly undertaken inside our supervised ICT laboratory suites.

We maintain zero tolerance for bullying, substance abuse, and exam malpractices. Active participation in spiritual activities and spiritual counseling services is strongly encouraged to cultivate God-fearing souls.
